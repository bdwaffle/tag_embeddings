Porn Tag embeddings

I've been scraping (responsibly) a popular porn tracker called Empornium for a while. Each torrent has a number of tags associated with it, including things like sex acts (cowgirl, missionary, etc.), studios/actresses (povd.com, riley.reid, etc.) as well as metadata for the specific release (1080p, 60.fps)

I took the torrents that I scraped (~7500 rows) and each tag associated with that torrent, and created word2vec embeddings of the tags, taking the list of tags for a specific torrent as a "sentence".

Click above to view the tnse embeddings for the word2vec output. Once it loads, click TSNE in the bottom left and wait for the visualisation to converge to something interesting. perplexity=30 seems to give nice results for me.

You can enter a tag to search for in the right to view the most similar tags (in the original 150-dim vector space)
