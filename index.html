Porn Tag embeddings

I've been scraping (responsibly) a popular porn tracker called Empornium for a while. Each torrent has a number of tags associated with it, including things like sex acts (cowgirl, missionary, etc.), studios/actresses (povd.com, riley.reid, etc.) as well as metadata for the specific release (1080p, 60.fps)

I took the torrents that I scraped (~7500 rows) and each tag associated with that torrent, and created word2vec embeddings of the tags, taking the list of tags for a specific torrent as a "sentence".

Click below to view the tnse embeddings for the word2vec output. Once it loads, click TSNE in the bottom left, click stop, change perplexity to 30, and click Re-run. then click stop after a couple hundred iterations (once it starts to converge to some interesting clusters)

You can enter a tag to search for in the right to view the most similar tags (in the original 150-dim vector space)

Some interesting tags:

  • lubed.com - related tags are other sites from the same POVD.com network
  • femdom
  • elsa.jean - most similar tags are actresses she works with often and studios she's made many videos for
  • gangbang
  • charlotte.sarte - identifies heavily.tattooed, goth as related tags
  • kink.com - most similar tags are ball.gag, rough.sex etc.
